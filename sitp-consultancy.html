SITP Consultancy

Summer Internship Training Program (SITP)

IMMD Laboratory offers Summer Internship Training Program (SITP) for B.E (ECE / EEE / Mech / MU / EIE / EICE / Civil).


Benefits:

  • To acquire hands-on training on the equipment
  • To learn basics of the equipment for synthesis
  • To develop practical knowledge
  • To understand the basic synthesis process of functional materials
  • To know the real use of the testing instruments

Course Duration: 15 days (conducted once a year)


Note:

  • Only limited seats are available. Book your seat via email: institutemmd@gmail.com
  • Payment should be made after receiving confirmation email.
  • Certificates will be provided to all selected participants.
