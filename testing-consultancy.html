1. Impedance Analyzer – LCR Meter

Instrument: HIOKI IM3536 LCR Meter

Year of Installation: June, 2023

Frequency Range: 50 Hz to 6 MHz

Temperature Range: Room temperature to 150 °C

Functions: L, C, R (ac), Q, tan δ, impedance, phase – Series or Parallel circuit

About Instrument and its Applications:

The impedance analyzer (HIOKI IM3536 LCR Meter) studies dielectric properties of samples across 50Hz–6MHz and temperatures up to 150°C. It provides Tan δ, Impedance, Cp, Rp, Rdc, Lp, Cs, Rs, Ls, Q-factor, Reactance, Admittance, Conductance, Susceptance, and phase data, from which dielectric constant, loss, complex permittivity, conductivity, and resistivity are calculated.

Sample Requirements:

  • Pelletized powder (⌀ ≈10 mm, thickness ≈5 mm)
  • Crystals (L ≈10 mm, B ≈10 mm, T ≈5 mm), polished on all sides
  • Samples will not be returned

Charges:

  • ₹700 per sample (up to 3 temperatures)
  • ₹100 extra for each additional temperature
  • Pellet Preparation: ₹100 per sample

2. Cyclic Voltammetry

Instrument: Admiral Squid Stat Solo

Year of Installation: October, 2024

Functions: Charge/Discharge (CC/CV), Chronoamperometry, Chronopotentiometry, Cyclic Voltammetry (CV), DPV, LSV, OCP Monitoring

About Instrument and its Applications:

Cyclic voltammetry (CV) is used to study redox properties via working, counter, and reference electrodes using a potentiostat. It aids in research on electro catalysis, biosensors, battery materials, pollutant detection, and drug analysis by providing insights into reaction kinetics and stability.

Sample Requirements:

  • Electrode materials must be properly heat-treated
  • Samples will not be returned

Charges:

  • ₹1000 per sample
  • Electrode Preparation: ₹500 per sample
